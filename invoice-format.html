Invoice format & design

Choose a sample (built-in or your own): it sets the active invoice design (visual theme + which fields print). Browse previews on Invoice samples. Custom designs are stored per entity in this browser’s database. Pair with Terms & conditions for legal copy.

Changing the selection loads that format and sets it as the one used for new prints.

Built-in samples cannot be overwritten. Change options or visual design and use Save as new format to store a custom copy, or switch to one of your formats and use Save.
